This Tiny Shift Makes a Big Difference- The Mindset Mentor

How often do you think about the words that you're using?

The power of our words are often overlooked. A simple change of words can really change how you act, feel and see everything around you. They can totally change the state that youâ€™re in.

Specifically for today, I want to talk about the "Have to" mindset vs. the "Get to" mindset. There are so many things that we have to do. If I ask you right now to think about a task that you HAVE to do later, how does it make you feel? Without even doing it, do you feel grumpy? Frustrated? Bored? Now think of a task that you GET to do. Do you feel excited? Happy? Joyful?

Especially the words we say to ourselves- including the little thoughts that pop up in our heads. C'mon, you know what I'm talking about! The voice that keeps pestering you about all the things you have to do, all the things left undone, and our evergrowing to-do list.
