The High 5 Habit

Stop it. Seriously.

I wanted to open this post by posing the question, 'Do you catch yourself critizing yourself?' but allow me to change my wording, 'How often do catch yourself criticizing yourself?'. In this era where we've all fall into the hole of hustle culture and chasing perfection, I don't think anyone could truthful say that they dont critize themself. But stop and think about it. How does criticizing yourself actually help you? If we know that being loved, encouraged, and celebrated feels so darn good, if it keeps you going and helps you achieve your goals, why don't you do it for yourself?

Remember, everything that you want to be, you already are, my friend.

Here's a list of quotes that I found empowering/insightful:

  • "A high five is so much more than a slap of a hand. It's a transfer of energy and belief from one person to another. It awakens something within you. It's a reminder of something you've forgotten "
  • "You can work hard while being soft with your soul."
  • "You can take chances, screw up, learn the lesson, without burying yourself in shame."
  • "You can have huge ambitions and still treat yourself and others with gentle kindness."
  • "You can face really hard and terrible situations in life and double down on optimism, resilience, and faith to get you through."
  • "I used to feel like I was pushing a boulder up a hill. Now I was leaving my bathroom every morning feeling the wind at my back."

    • Your relationship with yourself is the foundation of every relationship you have in life. So cheer a little louder for yourself, push yourself a little further.
